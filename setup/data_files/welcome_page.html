Welcome to the {website-title} website, a portal to holdings of the Texas Biospeleological Collection at the University of Texas at Austin. Maintained by cave biologist James R. Reddell and curator Alex Wild, this collection is the largest in the world dedicated to cave invertebrates.

Functions of this site include:

  • Browse the taxa found in the collection
  • Browse the locations where specimens were collected
  • View color-coded maps of the data
  • View seasonality and history graphs
  • Predict which taxa might be found on future visits to caves
  • Query for specimen data based on custom criteria

Future versions of this site will expand to include records from additional institutions and the published literature.
